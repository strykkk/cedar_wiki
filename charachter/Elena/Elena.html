Personal History

       When the leader of the Covenant Organisation faces trouble, they inquire about the whereabouts of their carrier strike group. When the United Territories Committee encounters difficulties, they seek updates on the status of the Eighth Squad of the Security Bureau. Thus, this elite special forces unit, the finest within the United Territories (and arguably the most formidable on the planet Nox), earned the moniker "Task Force Fleet". Yet while Earth boasts ten carrier battle groups, Nox possesses but one such squad. Elena, aged merely twenty, commands this elite force under the codename "Flag-Bearer". A resolute and astute leader, she maintains unwavering composure and decisive action.

       Towards her subordinates, Elena maintains the appropriate demeanour expected of a leader, yet she genuinely cares for the wellbeing and condition of every team member. She excels at unlocking their potential and boosting morale. This fosters trust in her judgement among her team, with younger members even idolising her. In external dealings, she maintains an unassuming yet assertive stance, actively securing the team's rights and operational autonomy. While certain factions within the Alliance perceive the Eighth Squad as a rogue unit of arrogant warriors, Elena's approach is one of restraint. She avoids confrontation, seeking only assurances and understanding within established protocols.

       Whilst rumours of the Eighth Squad's deployment were indeed sufficient to send many rabble-rousers fleeing in terror, Elena maintained that empty reputations served no purpose in protecting the team. The only things one could rely upon were thoroughness, caution, decisiveness and courage. She placed great importance on conducting exhaustive reconnaissance, gathering as much intelligence as possible and planning meticulously before acting. Once operations commenced, she would lead from the front, mobilising initiative to address any contingency, ensuring both victory and safety.

       Elena constantly balances undertaking arduous missions with safeguarding her team members and thus far she has managed this exceptionally well, never subjecting her team to significant casualties. Should the situation become utterly critical, she would most likely prioritise the team's safety. The Bureau's senior leadership is well aware of this: only the most crucial missions warrant the deployment of the Eighth Squad, yet rarely are these missions so vital that they necessitate the sacrifice of these elite special forces operatives.

       Elena's efforts to preserve the squad's integrity appeared excessively cautious to outsiders: for instance, though their numbers had dwindled to a point where all could fit in a single vehicle, Squad Eight would never do so. Elena was particularly intent on ensuring Claudia and herself travelled in separate vehicles, thereby preserving an experienced seed.

       Elena was selected for Squad Eight whilst still a rookie constable. The rationale extended beyond her exceptional aptitude and potential to encompass her outstanding sense of justice, ambition and restraint in the use of force. The space race plunged the United Territories into severe anarchy. Elena's home was ransacked, her parents beaten into disability by rioters, while she narrowly escaped harm due to her young age. This experience fostered a deep hatred for crime within her, fuelling her desire to gain the strength to protect herself and her family.

       When Elena first joined, Squad Eight was even more formidable than it is now: both frontline combat and support units comprised seasoned Alliance Special Forces elite. The team operated with seamless coordination and a protective, almost patronising attitude towards new members. Consequently, Elena spent most of her time assisting and observing, as her seniors judged she required considerable further training.

       However, Squad Eight was far busier back then than it is now. Perhaps because experienced Alliance veterans came too readily, the United Territories Council treated the special forces elite as a jack-of-all-trades and a propaganda poster, assigning Squad Eight numerous non-essential tasks. They even demanded the squad frequently make public appearances within the United Territories and the Republic to reassure the populace, going so far as to require Squad Eight to train the Republic's Defence Forces. Consequently, whilst returning from a Defence Force camp, Squad Eight's vehicle was overturned by a massive roadside bomb, inflicting heavy casualties among the team members, Elena alone survived because she had remained behind.

       Subsequent investigations revealed that the bomb had been the product of a violent clash between citizens of two knightly domains, originally intended for an enemy militia. The domainers' conflict had inadvertently harmed the Earth Gentlemen, bringing disgrace upon the knightly lords. Over two hundred militia members were consequently hanged. However, the remaining veterans of the Eighth Squad were so disheartened by the incident that they chose to withdraw.

       The Committee had no choice but to entrust Elena with rebuilding the Eighth Squad, whose conditions were that the Eighth Squad henceforth retain the right to refuse non-essential assignments and that she retain complete control over the Eighth Squad's personnel and operations.
