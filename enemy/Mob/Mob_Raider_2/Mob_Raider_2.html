Mob Raider 2
Basic Info
These refugees from the Republic were far luckier than their compatriots who had joined the scavengers they were able to live in Unity City and maintain a relatively decent life. That is, until they believed the rumors that Earthlings, including Marianne, would turn them all into slaves and even kill them.

Weapons
PM md.63 Assault Rifle

Behavioral Tendencies
Leading the surrounding enemy forces, they use powerful, mobile assault rifles. When attacked, they retreat and engage in a tug-of-war with our forces.
