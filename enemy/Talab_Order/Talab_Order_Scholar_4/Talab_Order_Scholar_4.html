Talab Order Scholar 4
Basic Info
These nuns were "scholarly wanderers" sent by the Holy Moon Order of the Kingdom of Walentia. They were attempting to complete their monastic degree reports by gathering information about Earth's civilization within the United Territory. In the official propaganda of the United Territory and the Republic, the nuns were portrayed as cunning and treacherous, henchmen of autocratic monarchy.

Weapons
Skorpion Submachine Gun

Behavioral Tendencies
Excellent mobility and field of vision; uses a submachine gun, strong in close-range combat and capable of mobile attacks; patrols the battlefield and possesses strong pursuit capabilities.
