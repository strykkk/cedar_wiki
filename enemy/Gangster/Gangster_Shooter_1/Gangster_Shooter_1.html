Gangster Shooter 1
Basic Info
These gangsters may come from the partially shut-down United Front factories or from idle young people. They are a major nuisance on the streets of United City and a thorn in the side of the United Front Guard.

Weapons
Mosin Nagant Rifle

Behavioral Tendencies
With extremely strong long-range output and wide field of view, they use sniper rifles with extremely high single-shot power, usually stationed in sentry towers, and will hold their positions to the death without ever moving.
