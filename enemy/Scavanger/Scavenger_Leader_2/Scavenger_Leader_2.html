Scavanger Leader 2
Basic Info
These people, originally from the Republic region, lost their means of survival due to the aftermath of the economic turmoil in the United Territory. They entered the United Territory and made a living by "scavenging" various industrial wastes that were very rare in the Republic. Rocket debris and "unclaimed" cargo sent from Earth were the most popular among them.

Weapons
RPG-7 Rocket Launcher

Behavioral Tendencies
Important firepower points on the battlefield, using RPGs capable of countering vehicles and fortifications, usually stationed in fortifications, not good at mobile warfare.
