Marianna Commando
Basic Info
These Earthlings are fanatical followers of Marianna, firmly believing that Marianna's "Nox economy" will allow them to make a fortune on Nox and become the next winners in this new gold mine of the United Territory. They consider themselves explorers who have conquered alien planets, and often act recklessly, causing serious damage to the security of the United Territory.

Weapons
PM md.63 Assault Rifle

Behavioral Tendencies
Leading the surrounding enemy forces, they use powerful, mobile assault rifles. When attacked, they retreat and engage in a tug-of-war with our forces.
