Summary
The landing pod carrying the Warrant Officer and others crashed into scavenger territory due to a malfunction and was subsequently attacked by scavengers. To protect the cargo, the Warrant Officer and others decided to make a last stand (even though Karula was the only one fighting). Meanwhile, Valena learned of the situation and prepared to go with the Security Bureau's 8th Squad to rescue them.
