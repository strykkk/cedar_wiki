"Joint Development Region"

In 1975, the Republic of Ruvila, established with the help of Earth, designated a piece of land at the mouth of the Motlava River on the northern shore of the Ruvilan Sea as a United territory to be jointly built by the Alliance and the Republic, as a sign of respect for its allies. Legally speaking, the joint development Region also belongs to the Republic's territory and enjoys a high degree of autonomy, just like the various knightly territories within the Republic.

Today, most of the United Territory is built in a terrestrial style, with meticulously managed farms and ranches. These are operated by large corporations.

The industrial and mining towns, along with their supporting residential areas, are scattered throughout the area, protecting the United Territory's capital, Unity City.

According to 1990 data, the United Territory had a total population of 2.1 million, with 1.3 million residing in the capital, Unity City. Over two generations, Earth immigrants, locals of different races, and their mixed-race descendants lived harmoniously in this unique land of modern civilization on the Nox Planet.

landing field
The landing site in the United Territory received the vast majority of personnel and supplies dropped from Earth. It was an open area surrounded by barbed wire, with soft soil suitable for the landing capsules to land.

Outside the barbed wire fence lies a restricted wasteland known as the "Landing Site Outer Control Zone." Because the landing capsule might deviate from its landing point and strike personnel within this zone, it is also off-limits to residents, just like the landing site itself.

old town
In the early 19th century, the Ruvilian people established a town called Salai within the present-day city of Unity. This town has since been preserved and renovated as a commercial and leisure area.

The dilapidated timber-framed and clay-walled houses were reinforced, the bumpy and rugged alleys were transformed into clean pedestrian streets, and the fortresses that protected the city and the stinking moat were renovated and dredged, turning them into beautiful riverside parks where citizens can take leisurely walks.

Gagarin Square
Gagarin Square is the heart of Unity City. The Monument to the Conquerors of Space, which stands in the center of the square, represents the spirit of the city: striding forward and bravely climbing the path to space.

Unite City
Unity City, the capital of the United Territory, is likely the largest city on Planet Nox. Earth's technology and Ruvila's culture are intertwined and blended in a race against time to build this city. The city is shaped by an ideal: in the not-too-distant future, the peoples of both planets will be united as brothers, without distinction.
