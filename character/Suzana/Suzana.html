Personal Report

       Suzana was the least visible member of KM Company's office in the United Territories, not entirely because she handled the "wet work" and needed to conceal her identity, but also because of her energy-saving lifestyle; she was unwilling to spend any time or energy maintaining a facade of social interaction.

       Suzana had long ago set her sights on one goal: to survive and save enough money to retire as soon as possible. She had no fond memories of her tribe and was completely unmoved by the rosy future promised by Schmalkalden. However, she was grateful to the Prah Organization for teaching her a way to make money. After years of training on Earth, she believed she had grasped the essence of Earth society: as long as you save enough money, you can live a happy and carefree life. Suzana's daily life was very disciplined; the Prah Organization instructors praised her for having "Schmalkalden-like precision": sufficient sleep during the day, training and equipment maintenance at night, and a perfectly balanced diet.

       A structured life is essential for conserving energy for the complexities of work: "company business" always presents obstacles, and those coveting the office's property and goods must be dealt with. Many commissions also come from clients: to prove KM Company's products are worth the price, Suzana must skillfully use various firearms to shoot through birds, armor, wild beasts, the apple on Tatyana's head, or even the skulls of the client's enemies. Suzana accepts all commissions with equanimity, carefully calculates the price for each step, and forbids bargaining; additional charges are required for unexpected situations.

       Stella was naturally dissatisfied with such a less-than-ideal assistant—but she also had to admit that Suzana's fees were fair and reasonable, and her work was of high quality and quantity. Without her, many of the more physically demanding tasks would fall to Stella. Therefore, Stella paid close attention to the amount of money in Suzana's account—if it was too high, she would have brochures from Earth for selling beachfront villas and tropical islands, to inspire Suzana's new vision of a wonderful retirement, raise her savings goals, and motivate her to do more dirty and hard work.
