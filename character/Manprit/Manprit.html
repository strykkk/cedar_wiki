Personal Report

       Manprit is a diligent and resourceful logistics contractor. She provides extensive material support for the Ruvira Mission's operations on Knox, while sincerely hoping that this support will last forever.

       Manprit was born into a poor immigrant family in Kalistan . Unable to afford expensive private universities, she chose to receive higher education through military academy. Her success was not only due to her aptitude for test-taking skills, but also thanks to the diversity enhancement program vigorously promoted by Lieutenant General McDonald.

       During her service in the Westphalian Army, she was an outstanding air assault officer, but retired quickly after meeting the minimum service requirement. She then used her connections with the Lt.Gen. McDonald to enter a Private Military Company ,and accidentally, she also provided protection for Lt.Gen. McDonald's daughter, Jacqueline, in various operations.

       Jacqueline, pampered since childhood, was oblivious to the cost of living, always placing orders without asking prices. This gave Manprit room to embezzle. On her invoices, a stainless steel coffee pot might cost as much as 60,000 pounds, a disposable napkin 10,000 pounds, and the logistics costs of shipping these supplies from Earth to Knox were even more staggering. Despite her skillful in enriching herself, the supplier lived a remarkably frugal life—she spent her nights in her truck guarding her goods, eating only flatbread spread on an old tablecloth, topped with chickpea or lentil curry paste, the color and texture of which were unbearable for the other members of the mission. Incredibly, Manprit spent most of the money she earned from the Ministry of Defense subsidizing her fellow countrymen to circulate in The United Vespuccian States through various channels. The family escaped their misery thanks to the help of their fellow villagers, so from a young age she firmly believed that she should work hard to earn money and help her country folks also run to The united State of Vespucci to earn big bucks in the land full of dummy

       In Manprit's eyes, Jacqueline and the warrant officer were both her benefactors and saviors of her fellow villagers. She hoped Jacqueline would forever chase after the warrant officer in Knox, and the warrant officer would forever keep making small, back-and-forth moves on Jacqueline, creating a constant back-and-forth and ensuring her business would prosper endlessly, while her fellow villagers could continue to enjoy The United Vespuccian States.

       They want to get rid of Bharat's discriminatory policies as soon as possible.
