Name: Claudia
Place of Origin: Joint Development Zone
Callsign: Ruslan (РУСЛАН)
Affiliation: Unified Armed Forces /EAST-08 (ОБЪЕДИНЁННЫЕ ВООРУЖЁННЫЕ ОТРЯД /ВОСТОК-08)

Claudia is the sniper of the Vostok-08, its cutting edge. A highly professional sharpshooter, she is a master of surveillance, stealth, and close combat. Calm, patient, and analytical, she’s earned Elena’s trust as a reliable backup commander.
Claudia rarely expresses personal thoughts or emotions. She strictly follows orders and adheres to mission plans. But if granted decision-making authority, she’ll choose to eliminate evil at its root—once she locks onto a target, survival becomes virtually impossible. To her, given enough time, every villain can be taken down—one bullet at a time.
Claudia holds herself to high standards and allows no signs of hesitation or weakness. She holds others to the same level of discipline. Whether military contractors, Ruvila Defense Forces, or fellow officers in the Security Bureau, Claudia generally considers them amateurish—but so long as they stay out of the way and let the professionals handle the critical parts of the mission, she’s willing to tolerate them.
In terms of military experience, Claudia is the most seasoned member of the Vostok-08. She was born into an agricultural technician family in the Joint Development Zone. In secondary school, she was selected for further education on Earth, entering the Izyum Suvorov Military School, then attending the Border Guard Academy. Due to her outstanding performance, she was on track for recruitment into the elite Vympel Unit.
However, the outbreak of the Union Civil War shattered that path. Instructors told cadets they could choose to return home—or “fight for their homeland.” Most, filled with patriotic zeal, chose the latter. But they were soon faced with a bitter question: Whose cause is truly just? Who is the rightful successor of the Alliance—or the one worthy of overthrowing its tyranny?
Claudia survived the war and returned to the Joint Development Zone, only to find her homeland in turmoil as well. The institution where her parents worked had been dissolved, its buildings abandoned. Their names weren’t listed among those who returned from Earth. Claudia eventually learned that many had gone to serve the Republic's lords to make a living—but tracing their exact whereabouts was near impossible...
It was during the reformation of the 8th Squad that Elena found this skilled warrior. Claudia earned her trust through unwavering professionalism and precision. Under Elena’s command, she fights with absolute dedication. She holds onto the hope that someday, she’ll reunite with her family—on a better, more peaceful Nox Planet.
