Personal History

       Koubel Mostovich is the combat driver of Vostok-08. She is also in charge of organizing the squad supplies and responsible for maintaining the outer perimeter security operations. Compared to the other full-fledged members who are highly active in the field and possess distinct, varied personalities. Korbel comes across as reticent and unassuming, quietly attending to her duties. She certainly has her own unique habits and principles, though she rarely reveals them to others; after all, her life experience has taught her that the more one expresses oneself, the greater the danger.

       Born in April of 1977 in a Samoyedic merchant family that migrated to the south of the republic at Knight Fief of Mostavian as the eldest daughter in the family with both her paternal and maternal families were merchant. She resident in Knight Fief of Mostavian (which has already become a Free City with population of 26 thousands) mix with Ruvilian, Circassians and Samoyedic immigrant where intermarriage among various ethnic groups is Common. Local immigrant children are required to herd Big-Eared sheep for their families as soon as they are old enough to walk, Koubel is no exception. Actually Koubel hometown gained road access during the period of the Republic's rapid development and is located near a major transportation road that went into the Zarrab Mountainous Zone, Koubel tends to see fully loaded trucks and long-distance buses drive past the village entrance when she was young, She grew an interest in the metal beast.

       Things change when she was 13. Due to Zarrab Secret War spillover effect and the crash of the Union economy, Ruvila fell into a state of turmoil, the town of Mostavian economy soon crash, small landlords and business owner begin to go bankrupt on a large scale, local poor peasant and landlord who wanted to do Inward Land Acquisition, yeoman who just move and bought some land fall into conflicts over newly cultivated hillside terraces and irrigation rights, escalating into large-scale armed clashes between opposing factions formed along village and local kinship lines, in which develop into cleansing of "Sojourner". In few years, Mostavian become the central of a miniature civil war, Koubel parent's business had to be stoped, Because they decided to escape after getting multiple life threating treat, one day during the night Koubel parents decide to bring Koubel and her two little brother onboard a long distance bus that was guarantee safety passage by the Union People Army, Wanting to search for a new life in the Joint Developement Region. Unfortunately, the vehicle was attack mid journey by an unknown group, Union soldiers onboard where killed while passengers which where mostly of Samoyedics and Circassians origin were shot and crushed by an armored tractor in the hand of the rioters, including Koubel Parents and her brothers, Koubel was hiding in the back of the bus, in a state of panic she awakened her EGO power, made the tractor engine stall immobilizing it, allowing her to escape.

       After she escape alone, Koubel traveled by begging, wandering for days, just as she was about to fall from the cold and hunger, she was lucky to be picked up by retreating Union Security Troops and was able to be rescued. Union soldiers pitying the kid, gave her food, teached her how to speak basic Slava and teached her how to drive trucks no matter how light or heavy. She becomame the mascot of the Union Garrison, helping the soldier clean the barracks, set up small stall at markets and use her ability to speak Ruvilian to help Union soldiers gather information on local markets. Afterwards, Koubel followed the Union soldiers retreat back to The Joint Developement Region and return to her home country, Koubel used any ways she could to become a Security Bureau non-regular auxiliary police officer, due to her outstanding driving skills that was way better than others, she was finally promote to regular officer after some hard times. In her opinion, even the workload for the Security Bureau officer was not high,but it was still a stable job, also learning how to drive could help her find work in a different sectors in the future.

       Due to never having a former education, Koubel Modern Ruvilian language skills was nothing special in her daily life, but she was always able to understand critical info's and details during mission.

       If there is anything bad to talk about her, it was that she is usually a bit too reticent, when it comes time to recognize achievements and hand out rewards, she never thinks to speak up for herself, leaving it to the captain and her teammates to stand up for her.

       Koubel could never shake off the though in her head, wishing to find out why conflicts happened back then in the small town and decide she should seek her revenge for her family and those who should not have died there. At first, she joined the Security Bureau just so she could feed herself, as time passed she saw the 8th mobile Unite,has the only collective she could entrust her future and her aspirations, after that the "Vostok-8" Task Force. After staying with the Warrant Officer and the others, She also became more outgoing and opened up to everyone. Due to the Vostok-8 squad driver retirement (he went back to earth to get married after the Battle of the Landing Zone) Koubel was transfer to drive "Sukhov"

Personal Report

       After the previous driver of the armored vehicle “Sukhov” returned home to get married, Koubel took over and began operating what had become the signature vehicle of the 8th Squad. In addition to the armored vehicle, the squad’s other Ural and GAZ trucks also rely on her meticulous care. While the rest of the squad finishes a day of training or duty and turns in for lights-out, Koubel may still be navigating the rural roads of the Joint Development Region, busy with supply tasks for the team.

       Koubel is the eldest daughter of a Samoyedic merchant family along the M12 Highway in Ruvila. As a child, she often watched heavily loaded trucks struggle to climb mountain passes en route to Zarab. But after a cascading economic crisis in the Union triggered chaos in Ruvila, Koubel lost her sheep, her hometown — and eventually all her family members. Wandering aimlessly after escaping from the barrel of a gun, she was finally discovered — on the brink of starvation — by a retreating convoy of the Union People’s Army.

       From then on, Koubel became one of the “children of the group.” She received her own aluminum meal tray in the Union army canteen, and soldiers from Earth taught her basic Slava, and how to put a pillow on the seat to improve her view while driving the Ural-375.

       As the situation worsened, the Union's garrison in Nox was gradually pulled back to the Joint Development Region, and eventually fully withdrawn to Earth. Once again, she lost her home. Koubel stayed behind in Unity City, where she took a job as an auxiliary officer with the Public Security Bureau. She could always breathe new life into armored transport vehicles that were running on their last legs due to spare parts shortages. Although she never received formal education, her talent, hard work, and attention to detail made her one of the best drivers in the Bureau.

       Koubel could understand the common language of Ruvila used by her comrades, but she couldn't speak it very well. Coupled with her taciturn but quick-witted nature, she always listened silently to her companions' needs and troubles, helping them to the best of her ability, and rarely expressed her own opinions.

       “The most memorable thing about Koubel to her teammates might be her unusual belief Along the M12 Highway, some locals believe that spacecraft streaking across the sky hold divine power, bringing unheard-of technology and new ways of life to remote regions.

       That is who Koubel prays to daily: ‘Rocket Mother.’

       Koubel firmly believes that Rocket Mama’s blessings helped her overcome the hardships and sorrows in her life.

       Every time another turbulent day arrives in Unity City and the 8th Squad rushes out of Sukhov’s rear hatch toward enemy-held strongholds, Koubel remains in the driver’s seat, solemnly praying to a Rocket Mother icon she made herself from a piece of fairing wreckage hanging above the observation window Hoping her comrades return victorious and safe.”
