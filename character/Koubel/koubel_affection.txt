Born in April of 1977 in a Samoyedic merchant family that migrated to the south of the republic at Knight Fief of Mostavian as the eldest daughter in the family with both her paternal and maternal families were merchant. She resident in Knight Fief of Mostavian (which has already become a Free City with population of 26 thousands) mix with Ruvilian, Circassians and Samoyedic immigrant where intermarriage among various ethnic groups is Common. Local immigrant children are required to herd Big-Eared sheep for their families as soon as they are old enough to walk, Koubel is no exception. Actually Koubel hometown gained road access during the period of the Republic's rapid development and is located near a major transportation road that went into the Zarrab Mountainous Zone, Koubel tends to see fully loaded trucks and long-distance buses drive past the village entrance when she was young, She grew an interest in the metal beast.

Things change when she was 13. Due to Zarrab Secret War spillover effect and the crash of the Union economy, Ruvila fell into a state of turmoil, the town of Mostavian economy soon crash, small landlords and business owner begin to go bankrupt on a large scale, local poor peasant and landlord who wanted to do Inward Land Acquisition, yeoman who just move and bought some land fall into conflicts over newly cultivated hillside terraces and irrigation rights, escalating into large-scale armed clashes between opposing factions formed along village and local kinship lines, in which develop into cleansing of "Sojourner". In few years, Mostavian become the central of a miniature civil war, Koubel parent's business had to be stoped,Because they decided to escape after getting multiple life threating treat, one day during the night Koubel parents decide to bring Koubel and her two little brother onboard a long distance bus that is guarantee safety passage by the Union People Army, thinking to search for a new life at the Territory of joint development. Unfortunately, the vehicle was attack mid journey by an unknown group, Union soldiers onboard was killed while passengers made out of from Samoyedics and Circassians was shot and crush by an armored tractor in the hand of the rioters, including Koubel Parents and her brothers, Koubel was hiding in the back of the bus, in the state of panic she wake awakening her EGO power, make tractor engine stall and immobilize it, this allow her to escape.

After she escape alone, Koubel make her way by begging, wandering for days, just as she is about to fall from the cold and hunger, she is lucky to be pickup by retreating Union Security Observing Troops and able to get a shelter. Union soldiers pity the kid, give her food, teach her how to speak Slav and teach her how to drive trucks no matter is it light or heavy. She become the mascot of the Union Garrison, helping the soldier to clean the barracks, set up a small stall at the market, sometimes use her ability of speaking Ruvila to help Union soldiers gather information on local market. Afterwards, Koubel follow the Union soldiers retreat back to Territory Of Joint Development and return to home country, Koubel use any ways she could to become a Security Bureau non-regular auxiliary police officer, due to her outstanding driving skills that was way better than others, she is finally promote to regular officer after some hard times. In her opinion, even the work for Security Bureau is not high, its still a stable job, also learning how to drive could her work in different sectors in future times.

Due to never having a former education, Koubel Modern Ruvila language skills is a nothing special in her daily life, but she aways able to understand critical info's and details during mission.

If there is anything bad to talk about her, she is a usually a bit too reticent, when it comes time to recognize achievements and hand out rewards, she never thinks to speak up for herself, leaving it to the captain and her teammates to stand up for her.

Koubel could never shake off the though in her head, wishing to find out why conflicts happen back then in the small town and decide should her seek revenge for her family and those who should not die there. At first, she join the Security Bureau just so she call feed herself, as time pass she see the 8th mobile the only collective to which she could entrust her future and her aspirations, after that is "Vostok-8" Task Force. After staying with Warrant Officer and others, She also became more outgoing and opened up to everyone. Due do Vostok-8 squad driver retire and went back to earth to get married after the Battle of Landing Zone Koubel was transfer to  drive "Sukhov"

In her personal life, Koubel is just a typical Samoyedic woman. Koubel wish to marry a good man, built a family better if both of them could take turn driving car to do business and have a lots of children. As for her personality, Koubel is deeply sensitive and guarded, finding it difficult to trust others. Yet, once a relationship is established, she will commits fully, embracing a mindset of unwavering devotion—prepared to follow her partner wherever life leads. However, having grown accustomed to a life of poverty and frugality, Koubel is live frugal life and quite "stingy". She always though her comrades is earning way higher and spend lavishly, with no thinking for the future. Because she saw how fiat money turn into waste paper, she save a lot of gold jewelry, reserve for emergency situation. In the mean time, Koubel is also expertise in caring that doesn't require money like making hot soup and traditional herbal tea.

Koubel rarely argue others, many think this is because her linguistic skills too weak to express objection. But in reality, it is not that she is unable to do so—she is simply gentle by nature. The best she can do is "I back to fix my car" and left

Samoyedic's Zoroastrian-style beliefs have lost their influence, replaced by a mystified, superstitious form of technolatry or cargo cultism. Although Koubel understand the principle of car and other kind of transportation vehicle under the teaching of Union soldiers. Yet, she consistently maintained and serviced the machinery with a superstitious attitude toward the equipment, example is that she always recite silently and pray to Virgin Mary rocket for the turbine after changing it's oil and have to perform hand crank start up as a ritual, which in result make her uncomfortable to new electric car model including 8th mobile's "Sukhov" armored car. If one dare to insult the Tablet of Virgin Mary rocket (made out of remain of a fairing", one would get the most serious threat they ever face. When she sees any rocket or spacecraft debris scatter anywhere around Nox, Koubel would burn some paper money if possible.
