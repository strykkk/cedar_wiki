Personal History

       Tiko serves as the machine gunner within the Vanguard Service Group squad, whilst also being an ardent enthusiast of super robot anime. She relishes embodying her innate strength as the super robot "Titan Cosmos", combating both imagined and real-world malevolent forces. Though not significantly older than her comrades, Tiko exhibits remarkably advanced physical development, boasting a volleyball player's well-proportioned, muscular physique that genuinely elicits envy from Murphy.

       Tiko's strength derives largely from her self-taught, singular EGO. Possessing immense strength, Tiko can lift objects weighing over a ton, treating anything nearby as a toy to hoist and play with. She can even effortlessly lift her employer Karula into the air with one hand, spinning her round. Perhaps due to her extraordinary physical prowess and the rarity of encountering difficulties in life, Tiko appears far more innocent and carefree than the other children at the orphanage, delighting in playfulness and mischief.

       Tiko's favourite game involves role-playing as a super robot in her imagination, particularly the titular mecha from her beloved robot anime Titan Cosmo. Even in real combat, she would shout lines from the anime, imagining herself transformed into an invincible mecha. She'd wield the mighty map cannon, the "Cosmo Beam Cannon", battling all manner of bizarre enemy craft. Since the giant mecha Cosmo was formed by the combination of three vehicles, thus, Tiko would sometimes reverently position Ruby securely upon her shoulders, declaring, "I shall form the torso!", to which Ruby would respond with commanding authority, "I shall form the head!"

       Tiko is extremely mindful of her image as a super robot pilot, so with the right approach, she can be persuaded to comply readily. In the past, Tiko was rather careless and prone to misplacing things, but after Ruby entrusted her with the "important task" of managing supplies and ammunition, she became diligent in her duties and has never misplaced anything since. Tiko also disliked eating vegetables. After Mei Mei admonished her that "not eating your vegetables properly hinders Cosmo Energy collection", she changed her ways. At every meal, she would now devour vegetables with astonishing vigour, tearfully reciting the charging progress of the Cosmo Cannon until "Charging complete!", at which point the vegetables would be finished and she would cheerfully tuck into her favourite meat dishes.
