Name		: Erika
Origin		: Joint Development Region
codename	: Joystick (джойстик)
Affiliation	: Cosmodrome Tactical Unit

To Erika, being a laid-back recon trooper is just the most basic form of social security a way to muddle through life in this dazzling interstellar metropolis. What truly defines a fashionable girl in Unity City is a brand-new hairstyle, a shortened uniform skirt, and sparkling little accessories on her shirt. Unless Julius personally drags her out, she’ll dodge both training and missions whenever she can.

In her eyes, everyone in the Unit is wasting their lives: Hannah wears her "don’t mess with me" face all day, Sylvia is always meddling in others’ business, and the funniest of all is Julius serious during the day, but curling up in bed with a romance novel and a pillow at night. Compared to that, Erika thinks her handheld game console is both stylish and cutting-edge.

Erika was born in a declining village in the Republic of luvila. Her father, a down-and-out knight, never let go of his pride despite their poor living conditions. Since she was little, he forced her to train in the family’s traditional martial arts. Bored stiff with her life, Erika couldn’t wait to leave home as soon as she completed the Republic’s mandatory 7-year education, heading straight for the big city of Unity.

Rushing traffic, whimsical architectural wonders, neon lights, raspy rock music from loudspeakers and all kinds of humans from Earth left her wide-eyed with amazement. Full of passion, Erika began reinventing herself, aligning her makeup and outfits with the fashion trends from Earth’s magazines. Besides shopping and karaoke, the quirky Earth-made electronic gadgets in shop windows especially tugged at her heart.

She worked exhausting jobs like retail clerk, movie ticket checker, and restaurant server, scraping and borrowing money until she finally bought that GAMEGIRL an eye-wateringly expensive purchase by Earth standards.

Big city life came with risks too one time, a group of drunk thugs started harassing Erika, thinking her small frame made her an easy target. In an instant, her long-dormant martial arts muscle memory surged back. By the end, the thugs’ faces looked like someone had used them as a paint palette.

Julius thought she had struck gold discovering a hidden young knight of exceptional skill and tried all means to recruit her under her command. Erika, imagining a job in uniform would be way more glamorous than waiting tables, agreed. Looking back now, it's safe to say: they both miscalculated.
