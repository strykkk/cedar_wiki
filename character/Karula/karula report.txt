Personal Brief
Karula is an employer at the Pioneer Service Group, a “vacationing tourist” from Earth. While she is usually carefree and unmotivated, once she gets serious, her resilience rivals that of a cockroach, fighting tenaciously to protect love, peace, anime, and adorable beast-eared loli characters.
Before coming to nox, Karula was a sharp and capable high-ranking white-collar worker with a high salary. By the age of thirty, her life was a mix of successes and failures—she had earned a dazzling degree, maintained a respectable income, and built an impeccable reputation. Yet, her spirit was drained by academic pursuits and work, leaving her with neither a lover nor close friends. Not even yoga, spiritual practices, vegetarian diets, or kombucha could heal her exhausted soul. A "master" once told her that her suffering stemmed from being unable to honestly confront her inner desires.
Inner desires?
Karula's only passion was anime, but her peers considered this hobby both vulgar and unsavory. Worse yet, she was an avid fan of beast-eared and loli characters. Restricted by Vespucci's societal norms and legal constraints, she could only pursue her interests in secret, further deepening her frustration.
One day, while routinely browsing newspapers she subscribed to for appearances, she stumbled upon key information about the planet nox:
The cost of living there was incredibly low, and its native inhabitants all had beast-like ears.
—Doesn’t that mean there’ll be a lot of beast-eared loli characters?!
Eager to witness them firsthand, Karula abandoned everything on Earth at an astonishing speed and boarded a spaceship bound for nox. She shared a cabin with two passengers—an adorable loli and a gloomy young person. Sharing space with an unfamiliarperson in the vastness of space made Karula slightly uneasy. Little did she know how these two companions would shape the rest of her life…
