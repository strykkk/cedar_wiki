A slender security officer with pink hair, green eyes, and fox-like ears, she possesses natural beauty yet is a master of disguise. She can easily make herself blend into the background—whether to observe the crowd and gather intel, or to secretly infiltrate areas and hunt for evidence of criminal activity. Her special talent is sensing where others are focusing their attention; this allows her to easily manipulate the flow of conversations and interrogations, steering the subject to provide the testimony she seeks.

Felice generally focuses on evidence gathering and often carries out infiltration missions alone. Since she possesses average physical strength and marksmanship—and harbors a psychological aversion to violence—she rarely collaborates with teammates assigned to direct combat roles, preferring instead to work closely with the Bureau's criminal investigation division. Her colleagues generally view her routine as follows: arrive at work, apply a disguise, head out, conduct investigations (location unknown), return, remove the disguise, and clock out.

Felice maintains a polite, professional distance from most colleagues, rarely revealing her true likes or dislikes. She strongly disapproves of Hannah’s rough, uncouth nature but never voices it openly. She enjoys teasing Julius, often eliciting cringeworthy, overly affectionate responses from him that make bystanders shudder—though Julius himself remains completely oblivious to the awkwardness.


Erika is Felice favorite "plaything." Erika envies Felice for her figure, looks, and makeup skills; combined with Felice's knack for psychological manipulation, it is all too easy for her to run circles around the young delinquent. Sometimes, she takes Erika along on investigations under the guise of helping her "grow up" or "broaden her horizons." In reality, she anticipates potential danger and recruits Erika—who excels at hand-to-hand combat—to act as her bodyguard.

Protecting the landing zone from theft rings and scavenger groups requires more than just overt force; it demands intelligence gathering—spying on their plans, identifying their fences, and unmasking their shadowy organizers. All of these tasks fall within Felice's scope of work.
