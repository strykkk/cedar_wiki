Dajana is Stella's assistant, and arguably the most important back-office staff member at KM Company's Nox office. When guests arrive at the office, Dajana is usually the first to come out and serve tea and water. Under the overly attentive reception of this lively and cheerful assistant, customers naturally feel obliged and lower their guard—only then does the cunning Stella make her appearance. If this process were reversed, Stella's difficulty in recommending products to guests would likely increase significantly…

Of all the Samoyed girls trained by schmalkalden, Dajana was the most innocent and carefree. She didn't quite grasp the theories of ethnic division and nation-building, and she didn't have much ambition for money or personal success. For her, everything was simply about "doing her job," and she enjoyed doing so and assisting her colleagues. Ultimately, after evaluation, the Prah organization determined that she would greatly contribute to team cohesion, so they assigned her as Stella's assistant.

Stella was naturally happy to enjoy her assistant's tireless service allowed her to focus her main energy on her respectable work. Suzanne was also quite satisfied with her; whether it was providing cover during retreats, preparing ammunition supplies, or balancing an apple on her head as a target to demonstrate firearms performance to customers, Dajana could complete all tasks with ease and without any hindrance.

Irmira, however, had some reservations about Dajana: the "artifacts" she painstakingly collected were often thrown away as trash by Dajana, or "recycled" into other decorations or ornaments. Every time she discovered something was missing from the warehouse, Irmira couldn't help but fly into a rage—but Dajana seemed to have an astonishing intuition: the things she disposed of were often ultimately proven to be worthless, common items, while truly precious artifacts were never lost.

Dajana is always so easy to order around and trusts others so readily—not only her colleagues but also strangers. This lack of vigilance makes one worry about her safety. Therefore, although Stella often uses Dajana as bait, she is always very careful to prevent her from being taken away.
