Joint Development Region
A special autonomous region on Planet Nox, jointly established by the Alliance, the Republic, and the Union under a cooperative agreement. While officially part of the Republic, it is governed by the Joint Development Territory Management Committee, whose members represent all participating Earth factions.

Republic of Ruvila
Born during the Ruvila Revolution, supported by Earthlings. It inherited most of the former kingdom's territory, namely the Ruvila Plains, a vast expanse of grasslands and forests. Its southern border lies the Ruvila Sea. The country is divided into six provinces, one capital special district and the Joint Development Territory.
