Personnel Report 614
Name: Julius
Origin: Republic of ruvila
Personal Briefing
Call sign: Spurs
Affiliation: Landing Zone Guard Mobile Unit
DECLASSIFIED
Julius is a striking figure within the Security Bureau: her uniform is always impeccably pressed, her boots gleaming, and her hair perfectly groomed. Regardless of the intensity of training or missions, she maintains a pristine appearance. As a result, she once appeared on a promotional poster for the Security Bureau, earning the admiration of many young officers.
As the captain of the Landing Zone Guard Mobile Unit, Julius immediately transformed the formerly lax and disorganized unit. She firmly believes that an excellent knight should inspire their comrades to constantly improve in both training and discipline. The team members, accustomed to a more relaxed approach, found it difficult to adjust. However, since Julius leads by example, they have no grounds for complaint.
Julius comes from one of the most prestigious knightly families of Luwira, but by her generation, the family had fallen into decline, with no land left to its name. From a young age, Julius vowed to restore her ancestors' glory, which is why she dedicated herself to studying Earth’s military science. In her spare time, she is always reading, diligently studying any Earth military literature she can find.
However, Julius seems to have failed to realize that some Earth books are purely for entertainment. Books and comics like
The Code of the Maiden Knight, 41 Sweet Military Rules, and An Ace Soldier Fell in Love with Me are regarded by her with the same seriousness as On War. Due to her rather eclectic learning, she occasionally quotes such works in a serious tone, reciting rather cheesy lines as if they were profound military maxims...
