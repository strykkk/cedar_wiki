Personal History

       This seasoned mercenary, capable of taking on a thousand men single-handedly, is short-tempered and vicious, possessing astonishing destructive power on the battlefield, earning her the nickname "Princess of the Fiery Tongue." In her daily life, she is extremely lazy and unwilling to be bound by military discipline. Currently, she is engaged in a cat-and-mouse game of wits with the overly serious and responsible new commander, Julius, unfortunately, she is on the mouse side.

       Formerly employed by military contractors, she now belongs to the Joint Development Territory's landing site guard mobile unit, which, according to her, is because the Joint Development Territory is very willing to pay, that's all.

       Hannah wasn't actually that belligerent—what she desired was a carefree life of vengeance and revenge, at least enough to bask in the sun and sleep soundly when it was warm. The life of constantly risking her life as a defense contractor had left her weary. However, she had also grown accustomed to gaining respect through displaying strength and arrogance, thereby securing a more relaxed and comfortable space.

       Hannah, upon joining the mobile unit, did indeed achieve her life goals for a time: she would get drunk at night, sleep all day, and single-handedly solve missions relying on her experience and well-tested animal instincts. Her superiors and comrades dared not provoke this uncrowned queen, instead relying on her as their primary fighting force. Unfortunately, the arrival of the clueless Julius shattered her idyllic life.

       Faced with a demanding new commander, she tried to use her old tricks again, but intimidation and threats were useless against the highly ambitious knight. In terms of strength, Julius was no less capable. Moreover, the new captain was working hard to discover her talent as a machine gunner, patiently guiding her training and urging (actually forcing) her to change her mercenary tactics, which were characterized by grand scale but wasted ammunition.

       Hannah remained sharp-tongued, but unable to openly contradict her superior, she resorted to underhanded tactics: pouring whiskey into opaque jugs or switching to vodka, so Julius wouldn't catch her drinking it. If discovered, she'd feign ignorance and deny everything. She also seized every opportunity when Julius was out on business to sleep in. Julius's new management style had improved the skills of her and her colleagues, but Hannah still didn't want to rely on her superior and teammates, perhaps feeling she would incur too many favors.
