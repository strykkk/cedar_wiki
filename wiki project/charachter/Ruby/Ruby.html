Personal History

       A loli-figured girl who feigns composure and cunning, prone to grandiloquent claims, styling herself "General Ruby". She asserts that in a past life she was a great commander and having inherited those memories, she possesses innate mastery of military science. When making decisions, she cites her past-life recollections as justification. In truth, all her military knowledge, be it sniping techniques or command and management methods, is gleaned from cartoons and films. Her current demeanour mimics cinematic generals, including addressing herself in the third person and holding lollipops as if they were cigars.

       Essentially still rather childish, thus easily revealing their true colours when faced with temptations (such as sweets) and prone to impatience in situations. Yet after such lapses, she swiftly recalls his role as a renowned commander, resolving issues with a leader's sense of justice, for instance, establishing fundamental duty divisions within the group. By instilling responsibility and pride through delegated authority, she encourages self-management among her companions, entrusting Tiko with safeguarding cash and supplies. When energised, they possess boundless vitality, yet grow drowsy once the momentum wanes, relying on the trust and protection of their companions.

       Ruby hailed from an orphanage in a border city of the Republic of Luvila. During the space race development era, this city garnered particular attention due to its abundant rare earth mineral deposits. However, following the dissolution of the Federation, the industrial upgrading programme jointly pursued by the Union Territories and the Republic came to a halt. Consequently, the city's municipal services deteriorated and the orphanage soon declined to the point where even three meals a day could not be reliably provided. At this juncture, the bold Ruby, along with her childhood friends Mimi, Tiko and Tina, ventured out into the wider world to seek their fortunes.

       The diminutive Ruby, possessing the sharpest mind, was universally acknowledged as the leader of the small group. As the nursery staff had repeatedly mentioned "Earth's sponsorship", the orphans naturally assumed Earthlings were the wealthiest and that the means to earn money lay, of course, in seeking out the United Territories.

       This band of daring orphans soon stumbled upon a "treasure trove" in the United Territories, a hoard of obsolete military supplies abandoned by retreating Alliance forces. Ruby's mind whirled and she swiftly conceived a scheme to turn it into a money-making venture. Drawing upon her fragmentary military knowledge, Ruby guided her companions in equipping themselves from head to toe. She established the squad's management structure and division of responsibilities, then proclaimed them a "well-trained mercenary group". They proceeded to peddle their "bodyguard services" to Earthlings newly arrived in the United Territories.

       Upon arriving in the United Territories, Karula, exhausted from being milked dry by Earth's conglomerates, immediately took a shine to this band of headstrong little "mercenaries". She promptly hired them as her personal bodyguards. Whether Karula actually needed bodyguards, well, that was another matter entirely.
